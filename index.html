My Projects

ShoMor

Shomor is a tourist recommendation system designed to enhance the travel experience for tourists visiting Goa. The system provides a user-friendly platform where tourists can search for various attractions based on ratings, ensuring they discover the best-rated spots suited to their preferences. Beyond just displaying popular places, Shomor takes personalization a step further by recommending similar locations across different categories such as beaches, historical landmarks, nightlife spots, or local markets.

This intelligent recommendation feature helps users explore diverse experiences while staying true to their interests. By offering insights into highly-rated destinations and suggesting related alternatives, Shomor simplifies trip planning and ensures that tourists get a well-rounded experience of Goa’s vibrant culture, scenic beauty, and rich heritage.

Camper's Haven

Camper's Haven is an innovative e-commerce application designed to cater to outdoor enthusiasts and camping lovers. This app provides a seamless platform for users to buy and sell camping tools, ensuring they have access to essential gear for their adventures. Whether it's tents, sleeping bags, cooking equipment, or hiking accessories, Camper's Haven offers a wide range of products to meet every camper's needs. Additionally, the app enhances the outdoor experience by recommending ideal camping spots based on user preferences.

These recommendations include picturesque locations for beginners, offbeat trails for seasoned adventurers, and family-friendly campsites, making trip planning effortless. With integrated features like user authentication, a shopping cart, and secure payment options, Camper's Haven combines convenience with practicality, making it a one-stop solution for anyone passionate about exploring the great outdoors.

Object Detection

The Object Detection app is a cutting-edge application designed to identify and provide detailed information about objects captured through a camera or uploaded as images. This app leverages advanced machine learning and computer vision techniques to analyze images in real-time, making it a versatile tool for various scenarios. Users can simply point their device's camera, take a picture, or upload an existing image from their gallery, and the app quickly detects objects within the frame. It provides accurate and descriptive information about the detected objects, such as names, categories, and relevant details.

The app's intuitive interface makes it user-friendly, catering to everyone from curious learners and researchers to professionals in need of quick object identification. Whether it's identifying plants, animals, everyday items, or even landmarks, this app offers a powerful and educational experience, bridging the gap between technology and real-world exploration.
